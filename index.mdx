## How it works

The skill runs entirely on your Mac. It queries three local SQLite databases — `chat.db` for iMessages, the Mail `Envelope Index` for emails, and the Address Book for contact names — and uses AppleScript to send messages through your existing apps.

<Note>
All data stays on your device. Nothing is sent to any external service beyond what Claude Code normally processes during a session.
</Note>
